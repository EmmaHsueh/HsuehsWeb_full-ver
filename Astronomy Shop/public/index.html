Bookworm Circle

Reading, to me, is a luxury. There are moments when I allow my emotions to take the lead, consciously diving into the vast sea of books and immersing myself completely in the world of words. In those moments, I can set aside worries, responsibilities, and competition, and lose myself in a realm of imagination and beauty. Yet there are always moments when reason reaches in from below, pulling me back, forcing me to wake from my reverie and face reality once more. Reading, then, is a rare indulgence — a fleeting escape that grants me a blank space to receive beauty that is not mine to keep.

Star Expo

An unforgettable experience that has become part of my identity. Leading a team of over a hundred in large-scale mountain observation activities, I engaged in professional astronomy courses and instrument setup workshops under the night sky and amidst natural landscapes. Serving as organizer, instrument instructor, and mentor, I learned from senior peers while taking on different roles each year. This has been a place that caught me when I felt lost, and an environment that nurtured me, teaching me the most when I felt unsure.

Beach Cleanup

A meaningful experience at Taiwan's northernmost middle school.I connected with nature and learned about tidal ecosystems and the struggles of intertidal creatures.I witnessed the impact of human-generated waste and the resilience of Mother Nature.Broken tiles, aluminum, and glass were worn smooth, becoming part of the ecosystem.I discovered my passion for marine ecology.Even small actions can help protect and nurture our environment.
